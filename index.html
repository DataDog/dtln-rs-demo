Instructions

Click the "Start Recording" button to start recording audio from your microphone. Click the "Stop Recording" button to stop recording. Click the "Denoise Audio" button to denoise the recorded audio.

Alternatively, you can load an audio file by selecting it from the dropdown.

The denoised audio will be played back in the "Processed Audio" section.

Note: The denoising process may take some time, especially for longer audio recordings.
